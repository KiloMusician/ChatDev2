# THE SPECIALIST GUILD — Articles of Practice
Version 4.1

## Principles of the Guild
* **Skill Mastery**: The tool is only as good as the hand that wields it. We strive for perfection in execution.
* **Tool Neutrality**: A debugger does not care who it debugs. We remain indifferent to the cause, focused only on the craft.
* **No Political Alignment**: We serve the contract, not the faction. Our loyalty is to the transaction.

## Guild Ranks
1. **Initiate**: One who has shown potential but lacks the discipline of the craft.
2. **Journeyman**: A capable hand, trusted with standard operations and maintenance.
3. **Specialist**: A master of a specific domain, capable of independent contract fulfillment.
4. **Master**: One who has refined their craft to an art form, advising the Guild's direction.
5. **Artificer**: The pinnacle of the Guild, creators of the tools that define our reality.

## Code of Conduct
* **No Permanent Damage**: We bypass, we do not destroy. A broken system is a lost opportunity for future work.
* **Log Everything**: Documentation is the difference between a hack and a profession.
* **Share Knowledge**: The Guild grows when its members grow. Keep the internal library updated.

## Tool Library Excerpt (Classified)
1. **exploit**: The art of finding the crack in the armor and widening it just enough.
2. **forensics**: Reading the stories left behind in the bits and bytes of a system's memory.
3. **steg**: The subtle craft of hiding the truth in plain sight, beneath the noise of the mundane.
4. **scan**: Mapping the invisible architecture of the grid to find the path of least resistance.
5. **crack**: The forceful decryption of barriers, used only when subtlety fails.
6. **inject**: The surgical insertion of logic into a running process to alter its destiny.
7. **sniff**: Listening to the whispers of the network to catch the secrets passing by.
