# DARKNET(1) — Terminal Depths Man Page

## NAME
  darknet — the underground market node

## SYNOPSIS

## DESCRIPTION
The Darknet market deals in contraband: exploits, zero-days, forged creds,
black-market augments. Higher-tier items require faction standing or story
beats to unlock. wallet tracks your anonymous darknet balance separately
from the Colony Bank.

## SUBCOMMANDS
  market          Browse available contraband listings
  buy <item>      Purchase a darknet listing
  sell <item>     List an item on the darknet
  listings        Your active sell listings
  wallet          Darknet wallet balance and history
