## DESCRIPTION
Tracks first-blood achievements. On TryHackMe, the first player to solve a
challenge earns a 50% XP bonus and a First Blood badge. In Terminal Depths,
first blood is awarded for being first to capture flags and solve challenges.

## MECHANICS
  - First solve of a challenge: +50% XP bonus
  - First Blood badge per challenge
  - Streak of first bloods: "Speed Runner" achievement
  - Monthly leaderboard: most first bloods wins special badge
