# RELATIONSHIP(1) — Terminal Depths Man Page

## NAME
  relationship — show the relationship between two agents

## SYNOPSIS
  relationship <agent1> <agent2>

## DESCRIPTION
Queries the Trust Matrix for the relationship between two named agents.
Shows their mutual trust, respect, and tension scores along with any known
alliances, rivalries, or hidden agendas between them.

Understanding inter-agent relationships is critical for the mole arc: knowing
who trusts whom tells you who could have helped the mole go undetected.

## OUTPUT FIELDS
  Trust      — how much agent1 trusts agent2 (0–100)
  Respect    — professional regard, independent of personal trust
  Tension    — active conflict / historical grudges (0–100)
  Alliance   — known shared faction or operation
  Rivalry    — known adversarial history
