  A total of 5 unique fragments are required to initiate the CONVERGE sequence.

## SUBCOMMANDS

  status    — show progress towards convergence
  analyze   — attempt to decrypt fragment metadata

  - 2 Fragments: Enhanced lattice visibility
  - 3 Fragments: Terminal ghosting capabilities
  - 4 Fragments: Partial reality override
  - 5 Fragments: Convergence Readiness
