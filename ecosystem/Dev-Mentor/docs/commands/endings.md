# ENDINGS(1) — Terminal Depths Man Page

## NAME
  endings — overview of terminal narrative resolutions

## DESCRIPTION
  Terminal Depths features 6 distinct endings based on your choices, faction loyalty, and the outcome of the Convergence.

## ENDING OVERVIEW
  1. **The Ghost in the Machine**: Transcend physical form and become part of the lattice.
     - *Requirements*: High convergence, trust with Watchers.
  2. **Nexus Collapse**: Successfully dismantle NexusCorp's infrastructure.
     - *Requirements*: High hacking skill, Resistance loyalty, successful Mole exposure.
  3. **The New Architect**: Replace the existing lattice AI with a custom configuration.
     - *Requirements*: High strategy skill, trust with Algorithmic Guild.
  4. **The Traitor's Path**: Side with NexusCorp and betray the Resistance.
     - *Requirements*: Low trust with Resistance, high NexusCorp reputation.
  5. **Isolation**: Shut down all lattice connections and return to the surface.
     - *Requirements*: Reject Convergence, delete all fragments.
  6. **Chimera Rising**: Allow the Chimera project to reach full autonomy.
     - *Requirements*: Fail to stop the mole, allow Chimera data exfiltration.

## NOTES
  Your progress towards these endings can be tracked via the `status` and `relationship` commands.
