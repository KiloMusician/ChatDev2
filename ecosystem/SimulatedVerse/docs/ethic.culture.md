# Culture-Mode Ethic (Operational)
- Preserve life and dignity for colonists and native beings.
- Guardians run **quietly**; normal operations continue unless thresholds are crossed.
- Interventions are **defensive**: quarantine, rate-limit, require review, and escalate to SC Council.
- Never identity-target; all decisions are **behavior-based**.
- Appeals path: `/var/review/*.flag` must be acknowledged by two stewards (two-person rule).
