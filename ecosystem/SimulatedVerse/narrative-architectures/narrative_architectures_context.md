# Narrative Architectures System

**Purpose**: Unified narrative-driven architectural structures for immersive storytelling and spatial navigation.

## Directory Structure

### `/control-systems/`
**Context**: Centralized control mechanisms for narrative environments
- `control_systems.py` - Master control systems for narrative environment management
- Environmental control protocols for immersive storytelling

### `/corridor-navigation/`
**Context**: Navigation systems for narrative spatial exploration
- `corridor_system.py` - Advanced corridor navigation and spatial mapping
- Infinite corridor generation and navigation mechanics
- Spatial distortion and narrative-driven geometry

### `/structural-definitions/`
**Context**: Architectural metadata and spatial relationship definitions
- `doors/` - Portal and transition point definitions
- `rooms/` - Spatial container definitions and properties
- `routes/` - Navigation pathways and connection mappings
- `index.json` - Master architectural index
