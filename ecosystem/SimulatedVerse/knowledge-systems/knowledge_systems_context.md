# Knowledge Systems Architecture

**Purpose**: Unified knowledge management, gameplay integration, and temple architecture systems for CoreLink Foundation.

## Directory Structure

### `/gameplay-integration/`
**Context**: Active Python systems for gameplay mechanics and knowledge management
- `Floor01_GameplayIntegration/colony_mechanics.py` - Colony management and gameplay systems
- `Floor05_KnowledgeManagement/documentation_system.py` - Dynamic documentation generation
- `Floor06_RooftopGarden/nusyq_hub_integration.py` - ΞNuSyQ consciousness integration
- `Floor06_RooftopGarden/autonomous_custodians.py` - Autonomous maintenance systems
- `Floor10_MetaOptimization/cascade_protocols.py` - Optimization cascade workflows

### `/elevation-systems/`
**Context**: Temple navigation and foundation architecture
- `elevator.mjs` - Inter-floor navigation system
- `manifest.yml` - Temple configuration and floor definitions
- `floors/01-foundations/` - Base foundation systems
- `floors/02-archives/` - Archive management systems
- `floors/03-glypharium/` - Symbolic and glyph management

### `/structural-metadata/`
**Context**: Floor ledgers and architectural metadata
- `floor_01/` through `floor_10/` - Individual floor ledger systems
- `index.json` - Master floor index and relationships
- Architectural metadata for temple structure

### `/archives-management/`
**Context**: Knowledge storage and retrieval systems
- *Future expansion for archive management features*

## Integration Points

- **ΞNuSyQ Consciousness**: NuSyQ hub integration for consciousness-guided knowledge curation
- **Autonomous Systems**: Custodial agents for knowledge maintenance
- **Gameplay Mechanics**: Colony management and progression systems
- **Documentation**: Dynamic documentation generation and management
- **Cascade Protocols**: Meta-optimization and workflow automation

## Temple Architecture

The knowledge systems implement a 10-floor temple architecture:
1. **Foundations** - Base systems and infrastructure
2. **Archives** - Knowledge storage and historical data
3. **Glypharium** - Symbolic systems and notation
4. **Research Labs** - Active knowledge generation
5. **Knowledge Management** - Organization and curation
6. **Rooftop Garden** - ΞNuSyQ integration and autonomous systems
7. **Observation Deck** - Monitoring and analytics
8. **Computing Core** - Processing and calculation systems
9. **Meta-Analysis** - Pattern recognition and insights
10. **Meta-Optimization** - Cascade protocols and workflow optimization
