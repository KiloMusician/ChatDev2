# Tic Tac Toe with an AI Opponent README
=================

## Introduction
This simple yet competitive game combines the classic strategy of "Tic Tac Toe" (naughts and crosses) with a challenging artificial intelligence opponent. Play against the computer in real-time, aiming to win without losing your own piece! The AI uses basic algorithms to challenge players but can be adjusted for varying levels of difficulty.

## Game Description
In this single-player game, you play Tic Tac Toe against an intelligent opponent controlled by a simple yet effective artificial intelligence (AI). Choose 'X' or the empty space as your mark and take turns to place it in a 3x3 grid with the goal of getting three of your marks in a row—horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. If all spaces are filled without a winner, the game ends in a draw.
